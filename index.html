View A House Address in Google Maps/Bing Maps

This super simple add-on takes a selected address and views that address in Google Maps or Bird's Eye view in Bing Maps, to help you visualize the address easier.

This is intended to be used with MLS site in Canada, to help you visualize an MLS listing.

Simply highlight an address on the page, right-click and select one of 3 possible view methods. (Street View doesn't currently work)

The original goal was to have it directly tied into an MLS page, but I haven't got the jetpack/jquery selection working, since I don't access to the docs yet.

Options include:

  • Google Maps Street View - view the house in street view
  • Google Maps Satellite View - view the house in Satellite View
  • Bing Maps Bird's Eye View - View the house with Bing's awesome Bird's eye view

Note, some views may not be available, based on where the house is located, and whether the views are supported in the maps
